NBA Award Predictions

Welcome to the NBA Award Prediction Tool

This tool uses machine learning to predict the most likely candidates for NBA MVP (Most Valuable Player) and ROY (Rookie of the Year) awards based on player statistics.

How to use:

  1. Select the award type you want to predict (MVP or ROY).
  2. Upload a CSV file containing player statistics for the season.
  3. Click "Predict" to see the top 10 candidates for the selected award.

For the best results, ensure your CSV file includes all relevant player statistics such as points, rebounds, assists, etc., normalized to per-game or per-48-minute values.
